Join the Team

We are seeking outstanding and motivated graduate students with research interests in genomics, transcriptomics, epigenomics, environmental exposures and cancer biology. Students would be enrolled in the Bioinformatics Graduate Program (https://www.bioinformatics.ubc.ca) or Genome Science and Technology Graduate Program (https://www.gsat.ubc.ca). Candidates should apply directly to Dr. Emilia Lim at emilia.lim@ubc.ca with a cover letter, curriculum vitae, transcript, and contact details for references.
